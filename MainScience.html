Science Quiz

Science is the systematic study of the natural world through observation, experimentation, and logical reasoning. It plays a vital role in everyday life and helps us understand how the universe works. Scientific knowledge contributes to advancements in medicine, communication, transportation, agriculture, and technology.

The Science Quiz section is designed to help learners strengthen their understanding of Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Environmental Science, and General Science. These quizzes are useful for school students, college students, and competitive exam aspirants.

Learning science develops curiosity, analytical thinking, and problem-solving abilities. It encourages students to explore concepts, ask questions, and discover new ideas.
