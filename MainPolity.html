Polity Quiz

Polity is the study of government, constitution, laws, political institutions, and the functioning of democracy. It helps citizens understand their rights, duties, and the structure of governance. Knowledge of polity is essential for students preparing for competitive examinations and for anyone interested in understanding how a democratic nation functions.

The Indian Constitution is one of the most important topics in polity. It defines the framework of governance, fundamental rights, directive principles, and responsibilities of citizens. Understanding these concepts helps individuals become informed and responsible members of society.
